About Fake News Detector

How It Works

This tool uses a machine learning model trained on a dataset of news articles to classify text as either real or fake news. The model employs natural language processing techniques, including TF-IDF vectorization and a Passive Aggressive Classifier, to analyze the content.

Accuracy and Limitations

While the model provides a prediction with a confidence score, it is not infallible. Fake news detection can be challenging due to evolving language patterns, satire, and context. Always verify information from multiple reliable sources.

Usage

Paste a news article into the text box and click "Check" to get a prediction. The tool is for educational purposes and should not be used as the sole basis for decision-making.

Privacy

No data is stored or shared. All processing happens in real-time on the server.
